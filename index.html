Welcome

My name is Andrew Aydelott and I had two dreams for what I wanted to do when I grew up. The first was to work for the FBI. The second was to become an archaeologist. After 20 years with the FBI, I have decided to take an early retirement and pursue my second career as an archaeologist to fulfill both childhood goals. I will be graduating from El Camino College with an anthropology degree at the end of the 2024 fall semester. After graduation, I plan on transferring to Cal State University Long Beach to earn my bachelor's degree.

When I have free time

  1. I plan travel excursions to see as much of the world as possible.
  2. I read as much as I can. The Count of Monte Cristo, by Alexandre Dumas, is my favorite book of all time.
  3. I create campaigns for my bar trivia turned Dungeons and Dragons group.

My favorite website is Atlas Obscura for it's unique travel recommendations.
